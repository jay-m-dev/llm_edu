# Non-Goals

- Real model training or fine-tuning. Rationale: MVP is an educational simulator only.
- Real inference libraries or GPU use. Rationale: keep compute lightweight and deterministic.
- Multiplayer or social features. Rationale: single-player scope for MVP.
- Accounts or authentication. Rationale: no backend requirements.
- Monetization or payments. Rationale: learning-focused prototype only.
- Backend services or cloud infrastructure. Rationale: local-first deployment.
- Real-world data ingestion pipelines. Rationale: scenarios are curated and local.
- Competitive leaderboards. Rationale: avoid online dependencies.
- Mobile-native apps. Rationale: web-first experience.
